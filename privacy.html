Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Nixicon. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our platform.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Account Information: Registration data, preferences
  • Project Data: Projects you create, settings, and content
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Process your projects and development requests
  • Send you notifications and updates
  • Improve our platform and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our platform
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Business partners for service delivery (with your consent)

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your data

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure servers, and access controls.
